🏫 CLASSROOM RULES

  1. Be respectful to everyone.
  2. Listen carefully when others are speaking.
  3. Keep your hands and feet to yourself.
  4. Raise your hand to speak or ask questions.
  5. Always do your best!
    • Listen, follow instructions, and focus on your goal.
    • Keep a flexible mind, open to new ways of thinking!
    • Have a positive attitude and help others.

Let's make our classroom a welcoming place to learn! 😊
