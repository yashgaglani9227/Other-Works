📚 My Favorite Subjects

I enjoy learning different subjects at school. Each subject gives me a chance to explore new things. Here are my top favorite subjects and what I love about them.

🔬 1. Science

Science helps me understand how the world works. I enjoy learning about space, planets, and the human body. Water is H2O, and Einstein's equation is E=mc2.

  • Biology: Study of life and organisms.
  • Physics: Study of matter and energy.
  • Chemistry: Study of substances and reactions.

📐 2. Mathematics

Mathematics teaches me how to solve problems. I like algebra, geometry, and statistics. I also like writing equations like a2 + b2 = c2.

  • Algebra: Using symbols and letters.
  • Geometry: Shapes and measurements.
  • Statistics: Analyzing data.

💻 3. Computer Science

I love learning how computers work and how to code. My favorite coding languages are:

  1. HTML - for building web pages.
  2. Python - for solving problems.
  3. JavaScript - for adding interactivity.

Learning is fun when you're curious!
